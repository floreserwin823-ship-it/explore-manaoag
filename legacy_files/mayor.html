Message from the Mayor

As the mayor of this beautiful and faith-filled town, it is my joy to welcome you to Manaoag — a place known for its devotion, history, and the warmth of our people. We invite you to discover our culture, visit our sacred church, and enjoy our local delicacies that reflect the heart of our community.

Whether you come for prayer, tourism, or fellowship, Manaoag has something to offer for everyone. Let us continue to work together to keep our town clean, safe, and welcoming for all visitors.
